Quantinuum completed its initial public offering on the Nasdaq under ticker symbol "QNT," offering 28 million shares at $60 per share for $1.68 billion in gross proceeds. The offering was led by J.P. Morgan and Morgan Stanley as joint book-running managers, alongside several other major financial institutions. The company plans to deploy these funds to advance its trapped-ion quantum computing systems and expand its commercial quantum computing platform capabilities.
