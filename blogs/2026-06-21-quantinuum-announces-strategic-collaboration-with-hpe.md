Quantinuum announced a strategic collaboration with HPE to integrate quantum computing with high-performance computing and AI infrastructure for enterprise customers. The partnership will develop hybrid reference architectures and validate application workflows combining quantum systems with large-scale classical computing environments, with Quantinuum providing quantum systems and expertise while HPE contributes HPC systems and deployment capabilities.
